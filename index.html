🕒 Schedule

Shotgun start begins sharp at 7:00 PM. Do not be late for tee-off.

👕 The Dress Code

Hawaiian shirts, sunglasses, and golf gloves are mandatory. Cool golf caps are highly preferable but optional.

⛳ Core Rules

  • 1 stroke = 1 sip of a pint or sippable drink.
  • 1 stroke = 1 "sook" through a straw. Counted the exact moment the straw leaves your lips.
  • You must wear a golf glove at all times while actively drinking.
  • Gloves can be swapped and shared between teammates.
  • Failing to finish a drink penalties your team with a heavy +2 strokes.
  • A hole-in-one is achieved if a drink is finished in a single continuous sip/sook. Anyone who achieves this gets a lei 🌺
  • Minigames cannot be ruled out
